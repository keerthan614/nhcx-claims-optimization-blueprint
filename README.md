## 📋 Overview

This project presents an **end-to-end workflow automation blueprint** that leverages NHCX's standardized FHIR workflows, ABHA-linked health records, structured pre-authorization, and real-time eligibility checks to transform India's claims processing landscape.

### 🎯 The Problem
- **15-30 days** — Average claim settlement time
- **₹450** — Average cost to process one claim
- **30-40%** of claims require re-submission due to errors
- **₹18,000 Cr** — Annual operational cost of claims processing

### 💡 The Solution — Six Optimization Pillars

| # | NHCX Capability | Before → After | Annual Impact |
|---|----------------|----------------|---------------|
| 1 | ✅ **Real-Time Eligibility** | 48 hrs → 2 sec | ₹1,500 Cr |
| 2 | 📋 **Structured Pre-Auth** | 3 days → 30 min | ₹2,000 Cr |
| 3 | 🔄 **Zero-Resubmission Claims** | 60% → 95% first-pass rate | ₹2,500 Cr |
| 4 | 🆔 **ABHA-Linked Records** | 5 days → 0 (auto-linked) | ₹1,500 Cr |
| 5 | 🤖 **Auto-Adjudication** | 30 → 200 claims/day/person | ₹3,500 Cr |
| 6 | 📊 **InsurancePlan Standardisation** | 25% → 5% partial denials | ₹1,000 Cr |

### 📈 Key Metrics
- **₹12,000 Cr+** annual operational cost savings
- **15 days → 15 minutes** claim settlement time
- **60% → 95%** first-pass acceptance rate
- **₹450 → ₹80** cost per claim processed
